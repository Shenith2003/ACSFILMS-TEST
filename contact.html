Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ACS Films.

How do I create an account on ACS Films? ▾
Click the "Sign In / Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar, then select the "Sign Up" tab. You can register with your email or use Google/Apple for a faster experience. Your account is created instantly!
Can I watch content in 4K Ultra HD? ▾
Yes! ACS Films supports 4K Ultra HD, HDR10, and Dolby Vision for compatible devices. You'll need a stable internet connection of at least 25 Mbps for the best 4K streaming experience.
How do I save movies to my Watchlist? ▾
Sign in to your account, then navigate to any movie or TV series page. Click the "+ Watchlist" button to save it. Access your entire Watchlist anytime via the "My Account" tab in the navigation bar.
Are subtitles and multiple languages available? ▾
Absolutely! Most of our content supports multiple subtitle languages including English, French, Spanish, German, Japanese, and Korean. Use the subtitle button (CC) in the video player to toggle them on/off and switch languages.
How can I request a movie or TV show to be added? ▾
Use the "Request a Movie or Show" form above on this page! We review all requests weekly and add the most-requested content to our library. Alternatively, email us at requests@acsfilms.com.
What devices are supported? ▾
ACS Films works on all modern browsers across devices — desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile. We support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Native apps for iOS, Android, Smart TVs, and streaming sticks are coming soon!
